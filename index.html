Sara Sigmundsdottir

Crossfit athlete

Ragnheiður Sara Sigmundsdóttir is born the 12 September 1992. She is an Icelandic weightlifter and CrossFit athlete

Known for her third-place finishes at the 2015 and 2016 CrossFit Games and her first-place finishes at the 2015 and 2016 Meridian Regionals and the 2017 Central Regionals.

She was featured in 2017 documentary 'Fittest on Earth: A Decade of Fitness'. She competed at the 2015 World Weightlifting Championships in the 75 kg category.

Here is a little more about Sara.

Her full name is Ragnheiður Sara Sigmundsdóttir, she was born the 12 September 1992 at Reykjanesbær, Iceland

She is 5 ft 8 inches tall and weight 152 lbs.

Here are few of her personal best:

  • Clean and jerk : 247 lbs.
  • Snatch : 209 lbs.
  • Deadlift : 342 lbs.
  • Backsquat : 298 lbs.

Here are her appearence on the past years :

  • 2013:She was 39th at the Europe regionals, was 169th at the open (worldwide).
  • 2014:She almost made it to the games, finish 12th at the regionals (Europe), and was 82nd at the open (worldwide).
  • 2015:Got the 3rd place at the games, was 1st at the regionals (Meridian) and 17th (worldwide) at the open.
  • 2016:Got the 3rd place again at the games, 1st again at the regionals (Meridian), 20th (worldwide) at the open.
  • 2017:was 4th at the games, 1st place at the regionals (Central), 1st at the open (worldwide).
  • 2018:37th at the games she had to Withdraw due to injury, got the 3rd place at the regionals (Europe), and 20th at the open(worldwide).
  • 2019:20th at the games, was 1st in the qualifier and 1st at the open (worldwide).
  • 2020:games didn't happened yet, but she was 1st at dubai competition and also 2nd at the wodapalooza and got the 1st place at the open (worldwide).
